About Me

Brazilian native, born and raised in Sao Paulo, moved to New York City at age 16 and worked in hospitality for the past 15 years. With a large knowledge of spirits Priscila created and design cocktail lists for well known restaurants in the City.

Bartending in New York was a great experience and it opened a lot of doors for her but, it was never her passion. She moved to Nashville with her fiance 6 months ago and started to work for a boutique hotel in the heart of Downtown where she works as an F&B manager.

She's currently enrolled at a coding Bootcamp a Vanderbuilt University hoping that she can change her career and continue to persue her dreams.
